The DAV Public School Library is a vibrant and welcoming place where students, teachers, and staff can come to learn, explore, and create. Our library offers a wide variety of resources, including books, magazines, newspapers, e-books, audiobooks, databases, and more. We also offer a variety of services, such as research assistance, storytime programs, and computer access.

Our mission is to provide our community with access to information and resources that will help them succeed in school, in their careers, and in life. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to learn and grow, and we are committed to making our library a welcoming and inclusive space for all. Our Team The DAV Public School Library is staffed by a team of dedicated professionals who are passionate about helping people learn. Our librarians are experts in information literacy and research, and they are always happy to help students find the information they need. We also have a team of library assistants who are responsible for the day-to-day operations of the library. Our Services The DAV Public School Library offers a variety of services to our community, including: Research assistance: Our librarians can help students with their research projects, from finding sources to evaluating information. Storytime programs: We offer storytime programs for children of all ages. Computer access: We have a computer lab with computers that students can use for research, homework, and other activities. Databases: We subscribe to a variety of databases that students can use to access information. E-books and audiobooks: We offer a collection of e-books and audiobooks that students can check out. Our Resources The DAV Public School Library has a collection of over 50,000 books, magazines, newspapers, e-books, audiobooks, and more. We also have a collection of reference materials, including encyclopedias, dictionaries, and almanacs. We are constantly adding new resources to our collection, and we are always looking for ways to improve our services. If you have any suggestions, please feel free to contact us. We hope you visit us soon!
